What is the perimeter? How to find it?

Perimeter is the distance around the edge of a shape. It is measured in units (such as centimeters or meters).

You can find the perimeter by adding the lengths of all the sides of the shape.

Its perimeter is 6 + 6 + 6 + 6 = 24 m.
